Our Heritage

Leyla's Boutique brings the centuries-old craftsmanship of Uzbekistan to New York City. Each piece in our collection tells a story of cultural heritage, artistic expression, and meticulous attention to detail.

Founded by Intizorhon Fataeva, our boutique celebrates the rich textile traditions of Central Asia while embracing contemporary design sensibilities. Every item is ethically sourced directly from master artisans, supporting local communities and preserving ancient techniques.
